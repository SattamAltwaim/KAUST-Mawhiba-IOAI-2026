COURSE SCHEDULE

Weekly Breakdown

Week 1: Apr 26 – 30

Foundations + Supervised Learning + First Neural Network
Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, train-test split, regression vs classification, decision trees, cross-validation, perceptron, gradient descent, PyTorch basics.

Week 2: May 2 – 7

Computer Vision + CNNs
Pixels & tensors, data augmentation, filters & feature maps, Conv-ReLU-Pooling, regularization, batch normalization, pretrained models (ResNet), object detection (YOLO), segmentation (U-Net).

Week 3: May 9 – 14

Sequences, Attention & NLP
Tokenization, word vectors, RNNs & vanishing gradients, attention mechanism, transformers, hands-on practice.

Week 4: May 16 – 21

Unsupervised + Modern AI
Clustering (K-Means), PCA & dimensionality reduction, self-supervised & contrastive learning, CLIP, GANs, diffusion models.
